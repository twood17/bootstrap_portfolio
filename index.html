About Me

My name is William Wood, I was born in Richmond, Virginia, and attended the local high school, Benedictine High School. I developed a love of all things computers at a very young age, starting on the NES with duck hunt and Super Mario Bros. This love later translated into wanting to know how programs, and websites worked. I started writing HTML in notepad, and tables with all CSS being written in to the HTML. Later, I started using the IDE, Dreamweaver, to make websites.

I currently attended school weekly at the University of Richmond: School for Continuing Studies Webpage Design Fullstack Bootcamp. This has been a marvelous experience, and I continue to look forward to progressing towards becoming a fullstack web developer!
